About Me

I am a graduate from UC Santa Cruz with a B.S. in Computer Science: Computer Game Design. I was born in New York City, moved to Santa Cruz for college, and have settled in Puyallup Washington to break into the game industry. 

I have enjoyed video games as long as I can remember and my career goal is to design and develop games for the next generation. 

Apart from my interest in game development, I am involved with the competitive fighting game scene. I enjoy both competing and helping out at tournaments while meeting new people!
